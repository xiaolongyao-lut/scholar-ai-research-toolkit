# Skill Card: 学术英语话语库 (`academic-english-discourse`)

Purpose: improve English academic discourse and Chinese-English academic translation.

Best for:

- Literature review drafting.
- Academic paragraph revision.
- Rhetorical move selection.
- Stable terminology across a draft.

Core moves:

- territory
- gap
- aim
- method
- result
- interpretation
- comparison
- causality
- limitation
- implication
- transition
- citation

Quality rules:

- Choose discourse function before polishing wording.
- Bind claims to evidence and scope.
- Preserve hedging and terminology stability.
