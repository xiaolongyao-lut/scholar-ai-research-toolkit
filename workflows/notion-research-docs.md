# Notion Research Documentation Workflow

Goal: turn discussions, notes, specs, and decisions into linked research documentation.

## Inputs

- Source notes or conversation summary.
- Target Notion workspace/page.
- Desired document type: wiki page, decision record, PRD, FAQ, or research brief.

## Steps

1. Identify the target document type before writing.
2. Extract facts, decisions, evidence, open questions, and follow-up tasks.
3. Link related pages instead of duplicating long context.
4. Keep citations, evidence refs, and local artifact ids separate from prose claims.
5. Write a short summary at the top and structured sections below.
6. Add status and owner fields if the document drives implementation.

## Output

- Structured Notion page.
- Linked source pages.
- Decision/evidence/task fields when relevant.

## Acceptance Check

The page should let a future reader recover what was decided, why, what evidence supports it, and what remains open.
